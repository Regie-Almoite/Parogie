RESTAURANTS & BAR

R efined pleasures at the table of a five Michelin-star chef

CUISINE DES VOYAGES
A contemporary restaurant, La Parôgiè Paris draws on culinary traditions from around the world revisited by chef Jérôme Banctel for whom it is a means of sharing with you his curiosity for other cultures.

Jérôme Baltazar

Executive chef at La Parôgiè Paris, Jérôme Baltazar, originally from Philippines, offers you a bold, pure and incredibly appealing style. Crafted from carefully selected produce, his dishes are masterpieces of diversity delivered in a delectably light yet bright burst of colors and flavors. On beautiful sunny days, you can enjoy lunch or dinner in the quiet green patio. Under the delicate shade of “silk trees” in bloom, this taste bud journey is a true Parisian summer delight.



PARISIAN COFFEE SHOP

La Fontaine De Belleville

Offers an unobstructed view of the Center Pompidou, its visitors and its effervescence. Located in the heart of Paris in the quivering district of Les Halles, the design of Café Beaubourg is raw and mineral. On the terraces, in the dining room or upstairs, you can sit in the large armchairs designed by the architect in all circumstances. From a Parisian breakfast to a business meeting, from a family lunch to a confidential dinner with friends… Café Beaubourg above all reflects a certain Parisian way of life!

GUERLAIN SPA

Under the 13th century vaults, the spa welcomes guests to a world of energising and relaxing possibilities:

Two spacious private rooms (one double) for exceptional treatments provided by the Maison Guerlain. Alone or as a twosome, guests can luxuriate in a refined setting, where ancient stones exude serenity far from the madding crowds;

A well-being area with dry sauna, whirlpool and gym equipped with treadmill, elliptical, rowing machine, bike, a weigh-lifting machine and free weights.
